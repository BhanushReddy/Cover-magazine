BMW 1250gs

By BMW - launched in 2019

The BMW R 1250 GS was first launched globally in 2018 as a successor to the R 1200 GS. It featured the new 1,254cc ShiftCam boxer engine, offering more power, better efficiency, and smoother performance. In India, the BMW R 1250 GS was launched in 2019, with multiple variants like the Standard GS and the GS Adventure to cater to different riding preferences.

BMW S1000RR

By BMW - Launched in 2009

The BMW S1000RR was first launched globally in 2009 as BMW’s entry into the liter-class superbike segment. It made its debut at the 2008 Intermot motorcycle show and was officially released for sale in 2009. In India, the S1000RR was introduced in 2010 and has since received multiple updates. The latest generation of the S1000RR was unveiled in 2022 and launched in India in December 2022 as the 2023 model with improved aerodynamics, more power, and advanced electronics.

Yamaha -R1

By Yamaha - launched in 1998

The Yamaha YZF-R1 was first launched in 1998 as Yamaha’s flagship supersport motorcycle. It revolutionized the liter-class segment with its lightweight chassis and powerful 998cc engine. Since then, the R1 has gone through multiple generations, with major updates in 2004, 2007, 2009, 2015, and 2020, each improving performance, aerodynamics, and technology.
